Data Science & AI Solutions FAQ

What services do you provide as a Data Scientist?

I specialize in machine learning engineering, MLOps implementation, and building custom AI agents using RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation). This includes everything from data cleaning to model deployment on AWS/GCP.

How do you approach a Machine Learning project?

My workflow follows a rigorous MLOps lifecycle: starting with business objective alignment, followed by exploratory data analysis (EDA), iterative modeling, and finally productionizing the solution with robust monitoring.

Do you work with LLMs and Generative AI?

Yes, I build production-ready LLM applications, focusing on fine-tuning, prompt engineering, and integrating Vector Databases (like Pinecone or ChromaDB) for scalable AI solutions.
